Good to know

Questions we get every week

How much does Colorbond fencing cost in Adelaide?

$120 per linear metre for a standard Colorbond fence, or $145 per metre with a concrete plinth underneath. Both include posts, rails, sheets, labour and cleanup. Your free quote confirms the exact figure.

How long does installation take?

Most residential fences are up in 1 to 3 days once materials arrive, depending on length and access. We'll give you a timeframe with your quote.

Do you remove the old fence?

Yes. Demolition and disposal of your existing fence can be included in the job. Just mention it when you ask for your quote.

Which areas do you service?

We're based in Playford and travel to all Adelaide regions: north, south, east, west and the Hills.

Can you match my neighbour's fence colour?

Almost always. COLORBOND® comes in a wide standard range, and we can run different colours on each side of the same fence, like the two-tone job in our gallery.
